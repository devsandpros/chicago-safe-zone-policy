# Privacy Policy — Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro

**Effective date:** April 10, 2026

## Who we are
Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro is developed by devsandpros.
Contact: devsandpros

## What data we collect
This app does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Your device's GPS location is accessed solely on-device in real time to detect
nearby safety cameras and generate alerts. Location data is never uploaded to
any server, never stored beyond the current session, and never shared with
any third party.

## Camera dataset
The app periodically downloads public camera location data from the City of
Chicago Open Data Portal (data.cityofchicago.org). This data is cached
locally on your device for performance. No personal information is included
in or attached to this data.

## Permissions used
- **Location (precise):** Required to detect nearby safety cameras while driving.
- **Notifications:** Required to display foreground service status on Android 13+.
- **Display over other apps:** Required to show camera alerts over your navigation
  app while driving.
- **Internet:** Required to download the public camera dataset.

## Third-party services
This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or
tracking services.

## Children
This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
any data from children.

Any future changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
