# Privacy Policy — Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro

**Effective date: April 10, 2026**

## Who we are
Chicago Safe Zone Alert Pro is developed and maintained by devsandpros.

For questions, contact: https://github.com/devsandpros

---

## What data we collect
This app does **not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**.

Your device’s GPS location is used **only on-device in real time** to identify nearby **safety zones based on public road safety data** and generate alerts.

- Location data is **never uploaded**
- Location data is **never stored**
- Location data is **never shared with any third party**

---

## Safety dataset
The app downloads publicly available road safety data from the City of Chicago Open Data Portal:

https://data.cityofchicago.org

This data:
- Contains **no personal information**
- Is stored **locally on your device only**
- Is used solely to provide safety alerts

---

## Permissions used

- **Precise Location**
  - Used to determine proximity to safety zones in real time

- **Notifications**
  - Required to display foreground service status (Android system requirement)

- **Display over other apps**
  - Required to show safety alerts over navigation apps

- **Internet**
  - Required to download public dataset updates
